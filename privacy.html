2. Overview

We take the protection of your personal data seriously. This privacy policy explains what data we collect when you visit our website, how we use it, and what rights you have under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Contact Form Submissions

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Preferred language
  • Service needed
  • Your message

This data is processed to respond to your enquiry. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual measures) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to enquiries).

3.2 Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 (Measurement ID: G-GJ1QXTBWL6) to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Approximate geographic location (city level)
  • Browser type and device information

IP addresses are anonymised by default in GA4. Analytics cookies are only set if you click "Accept" on our cookie banner. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent).

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting our site. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

3.3 Form Processing (Web3Forms)

Contact form submissions are processed through Web3Forms, a third-party form backend service. Your form data is transmitted to Web3Forms' servers to deliver the submission to our email inbox. Web3Forms privacy policy: web3forms.com/privacy

3.4 Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical log data (IP addresses, browser information) for security purposes. GitHub's privacy statement: docs.github.com

3.5 Fonts

We use Google Fonts (Poppins) which are loaded from Google's servers. This may transmit your IP address to Google. Google Fonts privacy: developers.google.com/fonts

4. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for as long as needed to process your enquiry and for up to 12 months thereafter for follow-up purposes. Analytics data is retained for 14 months (Google Analytics default).

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR) — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR) — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR) — request deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR) — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR) — withdraw cookie consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@docuassistgermany.de.
